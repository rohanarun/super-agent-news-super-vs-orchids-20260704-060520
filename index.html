Why computer use — and cache reuse — matter

From talking to doing

The market is shifting from chat‑only assistants to agents that can actually browse, click, authenticate, and complete multi‑step workflows. Google recently made computer use a first‑class feature in Gemini 3.5 Flash, underscoring this shift.

Source: memeburn.com

The cost of repetition

Many agents treat every run as brand‑new. For repeated operational tasks, that means paying the same cost again and again. Super’s computer‑use cache is designed so prior work can be reused.

Reliability over novelty

Reviews of Orchids highlight its versatility but also note reliability issues and the need to treat outputs as a starting point rather than production‑ready systems.

Source: makerstack.co
